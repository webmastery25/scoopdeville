About

Scoop DeVille is a Philadelphia ice cream institution! Started in 1989, Scoop DeVille has provided local and tourists with the most memorable ice cream experience you'll find! With our famous signature blends, we create custom made flavors on the spot. We use real ice cream and real ingredients to make unbelievably flavorful and textured ice cream, frozen yogurt, and vegan options. With ~20 base flavors and 80+ ingredients to choose from, your flavor combinations are endless. Choose from our guaranteed to blow your mind Signature Blends and Stacker Sundaes, or ask about creating your own!

Now offering our very own homemade fruit whip - compare it to water ice or Italian ice - available in all sorts of flavor options, or as part of a Whippie! Layers of fruit whip and ice cream.

Looking for a cake? We offer an elaborate selection of in stock ice cream cakes, vegan ice cream cakes, and cookie cakes. Plus we can custom make almost anything for you with a few days notice. Got a crazy idea? We can't wait to hear all about it.

Have an event, office party, wedding, or celebration coming up? We cater! Make Your Own Sundae Bars, Staffed & Scooped to Order Ice Cream Bars, Shakes, Cookie Trays, and More!
